Tales of the Night Sky

A Journey Through Mythology

Since the dawn of humanity, we have looked up at the stars and seen more than just points of light. We saw gods, heroes, monsters, and myths written across the vast canvas of the cosmos.

The ancient Greeks, Babylonians, and cultures worldwide connected these stars, drawing imaginary lines to preserve their most sacred stories. These constellations served as calendars, navigational guides, and storybooks passed down through generations.

From Orion the Hunter locked in eternal combat with Taurus the Bull, to the tragic tale of Andromeda, the sky is alive with drama and history. This interactive map preserves those ancient shapes, combining official astronomical data with the classic stories of old.
